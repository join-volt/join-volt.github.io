Welcome to Volt
Volt tracks one thing: how consistent you actually are.

Every day you complete your goals, your Momentum grows. Your Volt Score — your lifetime total — only ever goes up. It never resets.

Show up. Build something that lasts.
🎯
How it works
Set 3 goals each day. Complete them to gain momentum.

All goals done → +100 momentum
Half done → +50 momentum
None done → +0 momentum

Every point you earn also adds permanently to your Volt Score. That number is yours forever — even on bad days, your lifetime progress stands.
🛡️
Miss a day
Miss a day and you get a recovery window — your streak is frozen, not broken. Come back the next day and complete your goals, and everything continues as normal.

Miss the recovery day too and your streak resets to 0. Your momentum takes a hit, but your Volt Score is untouched.

Every comeback counts. Start again.
