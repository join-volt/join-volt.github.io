Welcome to Momentum
Momentum is built on one idea: consistency compounds.

Every day you show up, your score grows. Every day you don't, it decays. The longer you stay away, the faster it drops.

This isn't a habit tracker. It's a physics simulation of your discipline.
🎯
How your score is calculated
Set at least 3 goals each day. Your momentum gain is based on the ratio of goals you complete.

Complete all goals → +100 momentum
Complete half → +50 momentum
Complete none → 0 momentum gained

It doesn't matter if you set 3 goals or 10. A perfect day is always +100.
🔥
Your streak
Your day streak counts how many days in a row you've shown up and completed at least one goal.

End the day with goals completed → streak grows by 1.

Miss a day → see what happens on the next slide.
⚠️
Miss a day — Recovery Mode
Miss one day and you enter Recovery Mode.

Your streak is frozen — it won't grow, but it won't reset either. Your momentum is safe.

You have one day to come back and complete your goals. Do that and everything continues as normal.
📉
Miss two days — it gets costly
Miss a second day in a row and Recovery Mode expires. Your streak resets to 0 and momentum starts draining:
Day 2 missed: −10
Day 3 missed: −20
Day 4 missed: −35
Day 5 missed: −55
Day 6 missed: −80

The damage compounds. Coming back after a week is much harder than coming back after two days.
🏆
Clans & leaderboard
Create a clan or join one with an invite code. Compete with friends and family on a shared leaderboard.

Your goals stay completely private. Only your momentum score and streak are visible to your clan.

Nudge clan members when they go quiet. Let them know you noticed.
💡
One last thing
Momentum isn't about being perfect.

One bad day doesn't break you. Two does.

The app is designed to make coming back easy and staying away costly. Every day you open it is a day your momentum survives.
